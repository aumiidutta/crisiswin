Crisiswin

CrisisWin is a free, open-source platform that consolidates emergency helpline numbers from every country in the world into one easy-to-navigate resource. In moments of crisis, finding the right number quickly can save lives. Whether you're a local needing your nearest ambulance, a traveller unsure of the local emergency line, or a developer wanting to embed this data into your own app — CrisisWin is here to help. The platform is community-driven: anyone can contribute data for their country or state via GitHub. Together we can build the world's most comprehensive and accurate emergency helpline directory.
